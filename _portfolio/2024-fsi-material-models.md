I extended a flexible framework for structural simulations to cardiovascular fluid-structure interaction simulations, enabling new constitutive models to be incorporated into finite element solvers. This work investigates how material model choice and prestress affect patient-specific simulations, especially for applications focused on cardiovascular wall mechanics.

Advisors: Prof. Alison Marsden and Prof. Ellen Kuhl.
