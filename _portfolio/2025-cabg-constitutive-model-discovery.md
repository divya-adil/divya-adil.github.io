I am developing data-driven methods to discover constitutive models for veins and coronary artery bypass grafts using inflation-biaxial testing data from a collaborative rabbit study. The broader goal is to model the evolution of vein graft tissues and their components, with applications to understanding and preventing graft failure after coronary artery bypass graft surgery.

Advisors and collaborators: Prof. Alison Marsden, Prof. Ellen Kuhl, and Prof. Jay Humphrey.
