In Stanford Biodesign coursework, I worked with an interdisciplinary team to identify needs in pediatric asthma through clinician and parent interviews, literature review, and early concept development. We prototyped a gamified controller inhaler concept aimed at improving adherence and reducing asthma-related emergency department visits.
